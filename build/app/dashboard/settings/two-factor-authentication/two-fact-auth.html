Bittrex encourages the use of two-factor authentication

Two-factor authentication (2fa) greatly increases security by requiring both your password and another form of authentication. Bittrex implements 2fa utilizing Google Authenticator. To enable this feature simply download Google Authenticator on your mobile device and scan the QRCode.

Once you have linked the Authenticator with Bittrex, enter the 6 digit code provided.

Please back up your secret key. Resetting your two-factor authentication requires opening a support ticket and may take up to 7 days to address.
